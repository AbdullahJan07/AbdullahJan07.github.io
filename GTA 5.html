GTA V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an iconic open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Released in 2013, the game is set in the sprawling fictional state of San Andreas, which is inspired by Southern California. Players follow the intertwining stories of three protagonists—Michael, a retired bank robber; Franklin, a young street hustler; and Trevor, a volatile criminal with a chaotic streak—as they navigate the world of crime, personal struggles, and high-stakes heists. Known for its stunning graphics, expansive gameplay, and a richly detailed world, GTA 5 also features a dynamic multiplayer mode, GTA Online, allowing players to create characters, form crews, and participate in various missions and activities. The game's satirical narrative and immense freedom of exploration have made it a cultural phenomenon, maintaining its popularity across generations of consoles.
